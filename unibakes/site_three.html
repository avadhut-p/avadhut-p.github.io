How early should I place my order?

For custom cakes we recommend booking 3–5 days in advance. For simple cakes, cookies and savories, 24–48 hours is usually enough depending on the date and quantity.

Do you offer eggless and low-sugar options?

Yes. Most flavors can be made eggless on request. Low-sugar variants are possible for select cakes and tea-time bakes – share your requirements and we’ll guide you.

Where do you deliver?

We deliver across Pune. For Mumbai or other cities, select dry items (cookies / certain loaf cakes) can be shipped via courier with appropriate packaging.
